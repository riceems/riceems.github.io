The Rice EMS Captain is the highest-ranking student in Rice
EMS. The Captain is responsible for all patient care and
operational aspects of the department, overseeing the works
of the Lieutenants.
The Senior Lieutenant is the second-in-command of Rice EMS.
The Senior Lieutenant assists the captain in overseeing all
aspects of patient care, operations, and education offered
by Rice EMS.
The Shift Scheduler organizes the monthly Duty Crew calendar
and ensures that the campus shifts are appropriately covered.
The CPR/AED Lt. maintains the Public Access Defibrillation sites
on the Rice University campus and schedules CPR and First Aid classes
for the Rice community.
The Special Events Lt. meets with University officials to schedule
EMS for events, including athletic events, Baker Institute events,
and public parties.
The Operations Lt. keeps track of equipment and vehicle inventory,
and ensures that Rice EMS is always prepared with the necessary
medical supplies for any emergency.
The Education Lt. is responsible for managing the Rice EMT classes,
NSCI 281/282, and functions as the teaching assistant for the courses.
The AED team members assist the CPR/AED Lt. in conducting monthly checks
on Rice University's Public Access Defibrillation sites.
The BLS Class Coordinator assists the CPR/AED Lt. in arranging monthly CPR and First Aid Classes.
